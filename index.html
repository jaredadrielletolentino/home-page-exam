About All Time Low

All Time Low is an American rock band from Towson, Maryland, formed in 2003. The band consists of Alex Gaskarth, Jack Barakat, Zack Merrick, and Rian Dawson.

Known for their energetic pop punk sound and passionate fanbase, All Time Low has released multiple chart-topping albums and continues to tour worldwide, bringing their infectious energy to fans everywhere.
